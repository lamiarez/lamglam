Lamglam


LamGlam is a cosmetics brand created by Lamia in 2020. It's purpose is to help with self empowerment and self love through makeup and skincare. Starting small but looking forward to grow internationally, LamGlam is designed, produced & shipped from Lamia's offices. For all the women and men who wishes to grow their confidence, this brand is for YOU !
